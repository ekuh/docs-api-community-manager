Keep your instance up to date to take advantage of new and improved functionality in your community.

To update your API community:

. Open the ACM administrator interface.
. Open the *package update installation link* provided by your MuleSoft representative in the same browser session.
. Verify the version number and name of the release.
. Select *Install for admins only*.
. Select *Upgrade*.

This starts the installation process.

When the upgrade finishes, you receive a notification email.
