To show a live preview of the community's site, including APIs in *Preview Mode*, select the *Manage Communities* tab.

image::manage-communities.png[Manage Communities screen]

Open the site builder by clicking the *Builder* icon.

image::builder.png[Site Builder]

Click the *Preview* link in the top right corner.

To return to the site builder, click the *Back to Builder* link in the top right.

To view the live site, select the *Manage Communities* tab and click the *Open Community* icon.
