Select the *Manage Communities* tab and click the *Builder* icon to open the site builder. Click the *Preview* link in the top right corner to show a live preview of the community's site, including APIs in *Preview Mode*. Click the *Back to Builder* link in the top right to return to the site builder.

To view the live site, select the *Manage Communities* tab and click the *Open Community* icon.
