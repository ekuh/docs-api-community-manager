To show a live preview of the community's site, including APIs in *Preview Mode*, select the *Manage Communities* tab. Open the site builder by clicking the *Builder* icon. Click the *Preview* link in the top right corner.

To return to the site builder, click the *Back to Builder* link in the top right.

To view the live site, select the *Manage Communities* tab and click the *Open Community* icon.
