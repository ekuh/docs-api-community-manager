After you edit your community site, you can preview the new version to ensure that it meets your expectations:

. From the API Community Manager (ACM) Control Panel, click *Community Builder*.
. Click *Preview*.
. Examine your changes and verify that they appear correctly. APIs that are only visible to administrators will appear in this preview.
. Click *Back to Builder*.
