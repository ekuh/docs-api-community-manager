:imagesdir: ../assets/images/

After you edit and preview your updated community site, you can publish the new version for your users:

. From the API Community Manager (ACM) control panel, click *Community Builder*.
. Click *Publish*.

If your community is not already active, activate it:

. From the API Community Manager (ACM) control panel, click *Community Administration*.
. Click *Settings*.
. Click *Activate*.

After your community is published and active, view the live site:

. Open the API Community Manager (ACM) Control Panel.
. Click *Open Community*.
