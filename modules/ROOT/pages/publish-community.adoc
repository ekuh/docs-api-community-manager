:imagesdir: ../assets/images/

To make your community site live, open the ACM Control Panel, select *Community Builder*, and select *Publish*.

To make your community active, open the ACM Control Panel, select *Community Administration* and *Settings*, and select *Activate*.

To view the live site when the community is published and active, open the ACM Control Panel and select *Open Community*.
